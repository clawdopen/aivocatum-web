Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026  ·  Last updated: May 3, 2026

The short version: AIvocatum uses a Bring Your Own Key model. Your conversations go directly from your device to your chosen AI provider. DiMarcoDesign does not receive, store, or process your conversation content.

Who We Are

AIvocatum is made by DiMarcoDesign. Questions: support@aivocatum.com

What We Do Not Collect

  • The content of your conversations with AI models
  • Your API keys — stored only in the iOS Keychain on your device
  • Session transcripts or exports — local files on your device only
  • Location data or contacts

What We Do Collect

  • App analytics via Apple App Store Connect — aggregate, anonymized crash reports and session counts. No conversation content.
  • Subscription status — Apple processes payments and communicates subscription validity to the app. DiMarcoDesign does not receive payment details.

How Your Conversations Work

Your voice is converted to text on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition. That text is sent directly from your device to the AI provider whose key you have entered. The response returns directly to your device and is read aloud.

DiMarcoDesign is not in this path. We do not operate a proxy server or log your conversations. Your conversations are governed by the privacy policy of the AI provider you choose:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • xAI Privacy Policy

Home Base Feature

If you enable Home Base, session transcripts may be routed through a third-party messaging service (Telegram or Slack) to connect with a personal AI agent you configure. Messages are transmitted according to that service's privacy policy. DiMarcoDesign does not store, log, or process these messages.

Session Exports

Session exports are local files on your device and are never transmitted to DiMarcoDesign.

Data Retention

DiMarcoDesign does not store your data. There is nothing to retain or delete on our end.

Children's Privacy

AIvocatum is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes and reflected in the effective date above.
